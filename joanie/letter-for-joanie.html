Dear Joanie,

March 20, 2021 approximately, di man nakilala pero surprising kasi yung sinabi mo na nakita ako tung march, naalala mo pa name ko tung gatawag tawag sila or smthn. Welp, gusto mo trivia? Eto bigyan kita ng trivia. Alam mo ba na ang optimism mo, and the way you support me like try to backread. Ikaw yung tipong tao na gustong gusto ko makakasama na may walang todong suporta kahit na ako ay nasa ibaba na. You still managed to give me optimism. Even though it's not that big, at least you've given me some optimistic responses. Your optimism spread to me like,, hinding hindi ko alam paano ko ma describe pero grabe siya mag spread. Ano kasi, I am a pessimistic person and your optimism is a great help na sa akin. That's why ang ating bond is parang magkakapatid na. Actually, it's like we're siblings na. Like sibling bond gud. Kung pwede lang sana closer ang ating bond, better. Even though di tayo magkadugo, you're still one of my siblings because I treat you like my sibling na.

Joanie, I don't know how to thank you pa, the amount of help na binigay mo sa akin, even though you may or may not noticed, gachat palang ta, bulig na tuh sya. Kaya grateful ko that you're here na nakilala kita. And I am sorry kapag naging abala ako sa iyo. Minsan I can't control myself from being loquacious and I am really sorry like from the bottom of my heart, I am really sorry if my negativity and pessimism can handle yourself.
