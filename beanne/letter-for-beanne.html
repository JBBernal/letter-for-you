Juan's Letter for You

John Benedict Bernal 12/31/2021; 12 PM
Dear Beanne,

Beanne, actually, yung nakilala kita, I know you are a good person. I know you are a person na magandang kausapin (not to be sarcastic). Pero Legit, I am really grateful na nakilala kita because you are one of the best persons I have met. Legit ang bond natin parang siblings na tayo. I mean like, siblings naman tayo kaso indirect. Tandaan mo Beanne that I really love you to the point na huy, ang dami mong naitulong sa akin. Hindi mo lang na rerealize pero you also contributed a lot sa aking buhay. I know na introvert ka and ako extrovert and I really, really, really, really, like legit, I apologize because I became too loquacious. But you still changed me. Dahil sa iyo, I am a type na ng person na thoughtful. There's more pa iyan pero iyan lang ang parang highlight. You never fail me. Even though we went through ups and downs, you are still keeping that positive vibes. I am really sorry if I am too pessimistic kasi need ko kayo. Kayo yung mga taong optimistic na need ko. Tung hambal mo nga di mo gusto lagaw, I literally lost hope na makasama kita f2f or mag bond tayo physically. Pero yung sinabi mo na gusto mo magupod sa lagaw namon ni Bien et al., legitimate talaga ito na you gave me hope. I became a little optimistic. Pero hey, at least optimism is nandito na.

Ikaw naman, salamat talaga like as in, I don't know what to say more pa... pero I am really grateful na nakilala kita because legit hipuson ka pero pag magwakal ka, nalingaw ko kaistorya ka. Ako kasi, in order to become sane sa mga works like itong mga ginagawa ko ngayon, I really wanted to socialize. So sorry if nagiging abala ako sa iyo.
