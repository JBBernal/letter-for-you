Juan's Letter for You

John Benedict Bernal 12/31/2021; 12 PM
Dear Jandhale,

July 04, 2021 at 19:00 ang ating pinakaunang communication sa history ng ating kinabuhi, I cannot forget like wow ilang hours palang and we talked like we've known each other a long time na. I really appreciate those times where we laugh together, tas yung time na kayo ni Lovely naiintriga sa aking itsura kaya doon pa mas naging close friendship natin kasi tayo na ni Lovely ayun nag video call para sa face reveal, pero looking at the old days that cannot be returned, but remains as a memory that cannot be forgotten due to its very memorable state, magandang gawin ulit pero I think na you know, time goes by and siguro diyan lang sa time na iyan mapupunta. Pero of course, dahil sa iyong optimism and your enthusiasm, looking forward sa ganiyang times na hindi man sa gawin para lang sa ganiyang mabalik ulit, just fun lang. Kasi academics is ano kasi, occupied tayo dahil diyan. Pero sige lang, don't lose hope nalang.

Ikaw naman, I would really like to thank you for being with me along the way na malubak-lubak lalo na sa times where the day was not on the good side. I hope na magkikita na tayo sooner and I hope you enjoyed the year and sana maging maayos na ang lahat. Thank you sa ebriting.
